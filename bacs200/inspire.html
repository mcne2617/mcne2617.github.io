Kevin Hart

The only time you should look back in life, is to see how far you have come. -Kevin Hart

Kevin Hart inspires me because...

  • He is relatable. Although he is a successful comedian, he seems just like the rest of us.Hart engages with his fans often. He also manages all of his social media accounts personally.
  • He is generous. Hart donates to many charity organizations and even has his own nonprofit organization called Help From the Hart.
  • He is funny. Hart has never failed to make me laugh. He doesn't take himself too seriously. He often uses personal stories in this stand-up which I believe makes him likeable. Fans are able to relate to his failures, and are inspired by his ability to not get discouraged.
  • He is passionate. He is the true example of someone who has followed their passion. Hart was orginally a successful shoe salesman but quit his job to chase his dream of being a comedian. He even turned down an offer from Nike because he knew it wasn't what he was passionate about.
  • He is honest. Hart has made a few mistakes throughout his career, but he owns up to his faults and speaks on them and learns from them. Im 2013 Hart was arrested for a DUI. After his arrest he spoke out and said that drinking and driving is not a laughing matter and that his mistake was a huge wake-up call.

Kevin Hart is truly a genuine person. He built his career from the ground up and lives for his passion of comedy. I hope that one day I can pursue a career I am passionate about and that I can stay true to my character like Hart.
